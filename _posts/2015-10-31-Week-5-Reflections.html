My thoughts and reflections over the past week.

Health And Well Being

Diet and Exercise

This week I continued to cut back on the amount of junk food I was eating, and doing push-ups every morning when I wake up. I did not do any running or any other type of cardio however. This week I am going to do cardio exercise at least 3 days.

I finished the book Bullet Proof Diet, and it was an interesting read. Dave Asprey really micromanages not only the types of foods he is eating, but where they come from, how they are prepared, and what time of the day to eat them. I don't see myself religiously following his guidelines, but I do want to change my diet to follow his recommendation of a high fat (healthy fat) low carbs with lots of vegetables. I also want to start drinking his Bullet Proof Coffee. My hesitation is that it is a lot of up front cost to get the coffee, and the food is also not cheap, so without any form of income right now I am not sure what to do. As I write this, I realize that I am procrastinating eating healthy and am going to buy the coffee as soon as I finish this blog post, and make a meal plan for this week.
